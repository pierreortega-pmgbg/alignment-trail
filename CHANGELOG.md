# Changelog

## v2.0 — Cozy PM Campus (current, branch: v2-cozy-campus)

**Concept shift:** From terminal survival game to cozy top-down RPG.

The v1 framing was "PM as survivor navigating organizational chaos." V2 reframes it
as "PM as relationship-builder exploring a campus." Same subject matter, opposite
emotional register. The cozy format makes it more replayable and less stressful.

**What changed:**
- Canvas-based tile renderer replacing terminal UI
- Walk-and-interact mechanic replacing week-by-week event cards
- "Befriending" NPCs replacing resource survival
- Stakeholder Journal (collection) replacing endings/score screen
- Tuxemon tileset + Misa sprites replacing ASCII art
- XP and leveling system added
- events.js (v1 initiatives/events) no longer used

**What stayed:**
- The 12 stakeholder archetypes (same characters, now as NPCs)
- The PM scenario questions (content carried forward)
- Vanilla JS, no framework
- Vercel deployment

## v1.0 — The Alignment Trail (tag: v1.0-alignment-trail, branch: main)

Terminal-based text game. Oregon Trail format. PM navigates 26 weeks of
organizational chaos, managing Savings, Energy, Momentum, and Political Capital
while trying to align 5 initiatives.

5 alignment approaches (Data-Driven, Relationship-First, Executive Sponsorship,
Grassroots, Strategic Narrative) with different resource modifiers. 30+ events,
8+ endings.
